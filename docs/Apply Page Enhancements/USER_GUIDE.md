## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

Before you start applying:

1. **Complete Your Profile**: Fill out your profile with education, experience, skills, and languages
2. **Prepare Documents**: Have your resume, cover letter, and certificates ready (PDF format recommended)
3. **Review Job Requirements**: Read the job posting carefully to understand requirements

### Starting an Application

1. Navigate to the job posting you're interested in
2. Click the **"Apply Now"** button
3. The application form will open with your profile data automatically filled in

## The Application Process

The application is divided into 5 easy steps:

### Step 1: Personal Information

**What you'll provide:**
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number
- Country and City (optional)

**Auto-Fill Feature:**
All fields are automatically filled from your profile. You can modify any information if needed.

**Tips:**
- Ensure your email is correct - you'll receive updates here
- Use a professional phone number
- Double-check all information before proceeding

---

### Step 2: Education & Experience

**Education Section:**
- Add all your educational qualifications
- Include degree, institution, year, and grade
- At least one education entry is required

**Experience Section:**
- Add your work experience (optional but recommended)
- Include company, position, dates, and responsibilities
- Mark current positions with the "Currently working here" checkbox

**Auto-Fill Feature:**
Your education and experience from your profile are automatically added. You can:
- Edit existing entries
- Add new entries
- Remove entries you don't want to include

**Tips:**
- List education in reverse chronological order (most recent first)
- Be specific about your responsibilities and achievements
- Use action verbs to describe your experience

---

### Step 3: Skills & Languages

**Skills Section:**
- Computer Skills (e.g., Programming languages, Software tools)
- Software Skills (e.g., Microsoft Office, Adobe Creative Suite)
- Other Skills (e.g., Project Management, Communication)

**Languages Section:**
- Add all languages you speak
- Specify proficiency level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Native)
- At least one language is required

**Auto-Fill Feature:**
All your skills and languages from your profile are automatically included.

**Tips:**
- Be honest about your proficiency levels
- Include relevant skills for the job
- Don't overstate your abilities

---

### Step 4: Documents & Questions

**File Upload Section:**

**Required:**
- Resume/CV (at least one file required)

**Optional:**
- Cover Letter
- Certificates
- Portfolio items
- Other supporting documents

**Supported Formats:**
- PDF (recommended)
- Microsoft Word (DOC, DOCX)
- Images (JPG, PNG)

**File Limits:**
- Maximum 10 files per application
- Maximum 5MB per file

**How to Upload:**
1. Click "Choose Files" or drag files into the upload area
2. Wait for upload to complete (progress bar will show)
3. Files are automatically saved

**Custom Questions:**
Some employers add custom questions. Answer all required questions before proceeding.

**Tips:**
- Use PDF format for best compatibility
- Name your files clearly (e.g., "Ahmed_Hassan_Resume.pdf")
- Ensure files are up-to-date
- Answer custom questions thoughtfully

---

### Step 5: Review & Submit

**What you'll see:**
- Complete preview of your application
- All sections formatted as the employer will see them
- Edit buttons for each section

**Before Submitting:**
1. Review all information carefully
2. Check for typos or errors
3. Ensure all required fields are complete
4. Verify files uploaded correctly

**To Edit:**
- Click the "Edit" button next to any section
- You'll return to that step with all data preserved
- Make your changes and navigate back to review

**To Submit:**
1. Click the **"Submit Application"** button
2. Confirm your submission
3. You'll receive a confirmation message
4. An email confirmation will be sent to your email address

## Draft Management

### Auto-Save Feature

**How it works:**
- Your application is automatically saved every 3 seconds after you stop typing
- You'll see "Saving..." indicator when saving
- "Saved at [time]" confirms successful save

**Benefits:**
- Never lose your progress
- Continue later from any device
- No need to manually save

### Manual Save

**When to use:**
- Before closing the browser
- Before switching to another task
- For peace of mind

**How to save manually:**
- Click the **"Save Draft"** button at the bottom of any step
- Wait for confirmation message

### Resuming a Draft

**Automatic Resume:**
1. Navigate to the same job posting
2. Click "Apply Now"
3. Your draft will automatically load
4. You'll return to the step where you left off

**What's Preserved:**
- All form data
- Uploaded files
- Current step position
- Custom question answers

### Offline Support

**If you lose internet connection:**
- Your data is saved locally in your browser
- Continue working offline
- Data will sync when connection is restored

**Important:**
- Don't clear browser data while offline
- Reconnect to internet to sync changes

## File Uploads

### Drag and Drop

**How to use:**
1. Drag files from your computer
2. Drop them into the upload area
3. Files will automatically upload

**Visual Feedback:**
- Upload area highlights when dragging
- Progress bar shows upload status
- Checkmark appears when complete

### File Validation

**Before Upload:**
The system checks:
- File type (must be PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, or PNG)
- File size (must be under 5MB)

**If Validation Fails:**
- You'll see a specific error message
- Fix the issue and try again

**Common Issues:**
- **"File type not allowed"**: Convert to PDF or DOC
- **"File too large"**: Compress the file or use a smaller version
- **"Maximum files exceeded"**: Remove some files before adding more

### Managing Uploaded Files

**To Remove a File:**
1. Click the "X" button next to the file
2. Confirm deletion
3. File is removed from Cloudinary and your application

**To Replace a File:**
1. Remove the old file
2. Upload the new file

### File Categories

**Organize your files:**
- Resume/CV
- Cover Letter
- Certificate
- Portfolio
- Other

**Why categorize:**
- Helps employers find specific documents
- Keeps your application organized
- Makes review easier

## Status Tracking

### Application Status Timeline

After submitting, track your application through these stages:

**1. Submitted** ✓
- Your application has been received
- Employer will review soon

**2. Reviewed** ✓
- Employer has reviewed your application
- Waiting for decision

**3. Shortlisted** ✓
- You're in the final candidates
- May be contacted for interview

**4. Interview Scheduled** ✓
- Interview has been arranged
- Check your email for details

**5. Final Decision:**
- **Accepted** ✓ - Congratulations! You got the job
- **Rejected** ✗ - Unfortunately, not selected this time

### Real-Time Updates

**How you'll be notified:**
- Email notifications for status changes
- In-app notifications (if logged in)
- Real-time updates on the application page

**Checking Status:**
1. Go to "My Applications" in your dashboard
2. Click on the application
3. View the status timeline

### Status Timeline Features

**What you'll see:**
- Current status highlighted
- Completed stages with checkmarks
- Timestamp for each status change
- Notes from employer (if any)

## Withdrawing Applications

### When You Can Withdraw

**Allowed:**
- Status: Pending (Submitted)
- Status: Reviewed

**Not Allowed:**
- Status: Shortlisted
- Status: Interview Scheduled
- Status: Accepted
- Status: Rejected

### How to Withdraw

1. Go to your application details
2. Click the **"Withdraw Application"** button
3. Confirm your decision in the dialog
4. Optionally provide a reason

**What Happens:**
- Application status changes to "Withdrawn"
- Employer is notified
- You can no longer edit or resubmit
- Timeline shows withdrawal with timestamp

**Important:**
- Withdrawal is permanent
- You cannot undo this action
- You can apply again if the job is still open

## Troubleshooting

### Application Won't Save

**Possible Causes:**
- No internet connection
- Session expired
- Server error

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page and log in again
3. Try manual save
4. Contact support if issue persists

### Files Won't Upload

**Possible Causes:**
- File too large (> 5MB)
- Invalid file type
- Network issues

**Solutions:**
1. Check file size and compress if needed
2. Convert to PDF or DOC format
3. Try uploading one file at a time
4. Check internet connection

### Draft Not Loading

**Possible Causes:**
- Browser cache cleared
- Different device/browser
- Draft expired

**Solutions:**
1. Check if you're logged in
2. Try the same browser you used before
3. Check local storage hasn't been cleared
4. Start a new application if needed

### Can't Proceed to Next Step

**Possible Causes:**
- Required fields missing
- Validation errors
- Minimum requirements not met

**Solutions:**
1. Look for red error messages
2. Fill all required fields (marked with *)
3. Ensure at least one education entry
4. Ensure at least one language entry
5. Upload at least one file (resume)

### Status Not Updating

**Possible Causes:**
- Page not refreshed
- Real-time connection issue
- Employer hasn't updated yet

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Check your email for notifications
3. Wait - employer may not have reviewed yet
4. Contact employer if urgent

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does my draft stay saved?
**A:** Drafts are saved indefinitely until you submit or delete them.

### Q: Can I apply to the same job twice?
**A:** No, you can only submit one application per job posting.

### Q: What happens to my draft after I submit?
**A:** The draft is automatically deleted after successful submission.

### Q: Can I edit my application after submitting?
**A:** No, applications cannot be edited after submission. Review carefully before submitting.

### Q: How do I know if my application was received?
**A:** You'll receive an email confirmation and see "Submitted" status in your dashboard.

### Q: Can I withdraw and reapply?
**A:** Yes, if the job is still open, you can apply again after withdrawing.

### Q: What if I don't have all required documents?
**A:** At minimum, you need a resume. Other documents are optional but recommended.

### Q: How long until I hear back?
**A:** This varies by employer. Check the job posting for expected timeline.

### Q: Can I apply from my mobile phone?
**A:** Yes! The application form is fully responsive and works on all devices.

### Q: What if I make a mistake in my application?
**A:** Contact the employer directly to provide corrections or additional information.
